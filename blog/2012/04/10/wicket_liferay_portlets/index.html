Portlets With Wicket 1.5.x in Liferay Portal

Apr 10th, 2012

After Wicket dropped support for portlets and moving it to wicketstuff the settings in XMLs are changed: portlet.xml should look like 

	wickettest4
	wickettest4
	wickettest4
	org.apache.wicket.portlet.WicketPortlet
	
		wicketFilterPath
		/bla
	
	
		text/html
		VIEW
	
	
		wickettest4
		wickettest4
Note new portlet-class. Also, mime-type have to be text/html. web.xml also changed, notice filter class: 

	wicket.wicket
	org.apache.wicket.portlet.PortletFilter
	
		applicationClassName
		org.wicket.WicketApplication
	


	wicket.wicket
	/bla/*
	REQUEST
	INCLUDE
	FORWARD
And of course Maven dependencies also changed: 

	org.apache.wicket
	wicket-core
	${wicket.version}


	org.apache.portals.bridges
	portals-bridges-common
	2.0


	org.wicketstuff
	wicketstuff-portlet
	${wicket.version}
